Know Us More!

Lab Master S.A.R.L. is a training and consultancy provider for all types of laboratories, as well as distributor of quality control testing technologies, kits and consumables for local & international manufacturers of food, water, chemicals & more. Our services include provision of training. laboratory development towards accreditation, consultancy services, design, marketing, installation and maintenance of equipment, in addition to provision of technical support to laboratories.

Lab Master is a committed service-oriented company that applies its expertise to seek novel technologies worldwide and delivers integrated scientific solutions. We offer the best services and high-quality products from leading companies to improve the work of industrial quality control specialists, governmental reference laboratories and researchers in Lebanon.

OUR VISION

New developments and technologies are rapidly revolutionizing the research and laboratory market. To stay ahead of these fast-paced changes, Lab Master collaborates closely with world-leading manufacturers, offering not just innovative products but also high-level consultancy services across all laboratory scopes in Lebanon and the Middle East region.

OUR MISSION

Lab Master aims at providing comprehensive support to laboratories in establishing, improving and demonstrating their reliability and competence to their customers and the authoritative bodies. At Lab Master, we focus our attention, primarily and constantly, on our customers' work through the values, roles and responsibilities Lab Master encompasses.
